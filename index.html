About

My first job in the commercial photography world was a month spent trying to beautify barely sewn together prototypes and other samples for that athletic shoe company on the West side of town. Beauty shots on film, no joke. It came naturally to me and within two weeks I had a full-time job at that studio. It was 1997 and after a few years moving up the ranks and hitting the ceiling there, I took my education and became a freelance photo stylist.


I've been called upon by a variety of clients and shooters to do a variety of tasks as a stylist. Interiors, wardrobe, product tabletop, on figure, soft goods, and a lot of propping. That's what you'll find in my bags. Cost efficient and a quick problem solver, I feel very strongly that communication and openness to what can be done on set are the keys to providing a client with great images. Having fun in the process certainly doesn't hurt either.


-Kevin
